Welcome to the latest in innovative chat experiences.

We backtrack to the beginning of the Covid-19 pandemic, when a vast majority of the world from all walks of life had turned to social platforms in search of entertainment and human engagement. Just as nature falls into place, great minds among the billions united to collaborate towards the creation of cmx. A platform by chatters, for chatters has become an effective communication tool for diverse, attractive and talented minds to share laughs, collaborate, or simply lounge and engage with others. Our core values include full anonymity, giving anyone the platform to talk and let thoughts run free- without censorship from our end. A basic code of conduct is accommodated to ensure a safe environment. As the community grows, the scope of conversations are endless and the vast array of personalities have become a fun source of entertainment. The users within the organization continue to team on various projects while finding creative and intelligent people along the way. New members continue to join, contributing song recommendations, jokes, and even advice. Bonds and friendships continue to grow strong. Minglers mingle and the coffee breakers chat, the influencers influence and the relationships formed are just too many to count. Our chat software includes many features and options such as listening to music while chatting and heaps more through our VIP feature access. We support all mobile, tablet and desktop devices with no downloads. The site commits itself to the vision’s holistic development and will continue to unify engaging and innovative souls from across the globe toward a promising and fulfilling experience.
